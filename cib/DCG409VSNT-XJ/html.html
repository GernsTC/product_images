The DEWALT DCG409VSNT is a powerful and versatile 125mm angle grinder, designed for professionals who demand precision and control. Its standout feature is the Variable Speed Control, allowing you to adjust the speed to suit different materials and applications, making it ideal for everything from delicate surface finishing to aggressive material removal. PACK ID Technology enhances power when paired with an XR FLEXVOLT battery, while safety features like an electronic brake and clutch provide confidence during use.

Key Features:

  • Variable Speed Control – Adjust speed for maximum precision and versatility across different tasks.
  • 18V Brushless Motor – Delivers efficient power, longer runtime, and durability.
  • Electronic Brake – Stops the wheel quickly for enhanced safety.
  • Electronic Clutch – Reduces kickback reaction in case of a pinch or stall.
  • Wireless Tool Control (WTC) – Pairs with compatible dust extractors for a cleaner workspace.
  • Mesh Debris Covers – Prevents dust from entering the motor, extending tool life.
  • Rubber Overmould Grip – Provides comfort and superior handling.
  • 2-Position Side Handle – Enhances control and adaptability for various applications.
  • Electronic Soft Start – Prevents sudden jerks for smooth operation.

Applications of a Variable Speed Angle Grinder

The Variable Speed Control makes this grinder ideal for a wide range of applications, including:

  • Metal Grinding & Cutting – Lower speeds reduce heat buildup and extend disc life.
  • Weld Removal & Polishing – Adjust the speed for precise, smooth finishing.
  • Masonry & Concrete Work – Control speed for delicate grinding or aggressive material removal.
  • Paint & Rust Removal – Slower speeds help avoid surface damage.
  • Fabrication & Automotive Work – Perfect for fine-tuning welds, shaping metal, or bodywork repairs.

Whether you need high-speed power or controlled finesse, the DEWALT DCG409VSNT-XJ adapts to your needs, making it a must-have for professionals in construction, metalworking, and automotive industries.
