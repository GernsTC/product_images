The ADA Wall Scanner 80 is a compact, easy-to-use multi-surface detector designed to locate ferrous and non-ferrous metals, live electrical wires, and wooden structures behind walls. Ideal for renovation, installation, or DIY tasks, it helps prevent accidental drilling into hidden objects.

With automatic calibration in all modes, the tool ensures consistent and accurate results. Visual and audio alerts clearly indicate detection status—red for objects found, green for safe zones. A large digital display provides clear feedback, while its shock-proof casing and treaded sides offer durability and grip on the job.

Key Features:

  • Detects magnetic metal up to 80 mm
  • Detects non-magnetic metal up to 60 mm
  • Live wire detection depth: 50 mm
  • Wood structure detection up to 20 mm
  • Simple user interface with auto-calibration
  • LED and sound indicators for fast identification
  • Shock-resistant housing with ergonomic grip
  • Auto shut-down after 5 minutes to save power
  • Powered by a 9V alkaline battery, offering up to 6 hours of runtime
