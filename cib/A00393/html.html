Designed for tilt measurement of surfaces. Built-in magnets allow you to set the instrument on steel. The readable digital LCD display shows deviation from horizontal/vertical. It is reliable thanks to the Automatic calibration and built-in bubble levels serves as a double check.

There is HOLD mode for convenient operation. Use this mode to hold results on the display and the function of the backlight. The level beeps an alarm when the results approach to 0 and 90 degrees.

Functions

  • Automatic calibration
  • Data can be displayed in degrees, mm/m, %, in/ft.
  • LCD display with backlight
  • Magnets on the bottom
  • Automatic shut-off
