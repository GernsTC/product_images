The ADA Armo 2D Green Professional Edition is a compact and reliable self-leveling line laser designed for accurate marking during home renovations, light construction, and interior installation tasks. It projects a bright green horizontal and vertical line that intersect at 90 degrees, helping you work faster and with greater precision.

This model includes an elevating aluminium tripod for easy height adjustments, allowing flexible placement in any workspace. With features like tilt mode, external power capability, and a highly visible green laser beam, it's a practical tool for both indoor and outdoor use.

Key features

  • Green laser visibility: Bright green beam is more visible than red in bright or outdoor environments
  • Automatic self-leveling: Levels within ±4°, with blinking alert for out-of-range positioning
  • Flexible line selection: Choose horizontal, vertical, or cross-line projection with a single button
  • Overhead vertical projection: Vertical beam extends over the device for full-room coverage
  • Extended working range: Up to 20 metres, or 40–70 metres with a receiver in receiver mode
  • Tilt mode: Project lines at an angle for staircases or sloped surfaces
  • Power options: Works with AA batteries, rechargeable batteries, or mains power via charger
  • Durable and grippy housing: Ribbed design provides secure handling and jobsite durability
  • Magnetic mounting: Built-in neodymium magnets attach to metal surfaces like drywall framing
  • Tripod included: Lightweight aluminium tripod with adjustable height from 35 to 110 cm, plus a 25 cm lift range
