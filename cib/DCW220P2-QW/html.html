Designed for efficiency, durability, and dust control, this first-ever 18V XR cordless 75 x 533mm belt sander is the ultimate tool for demanding sanding applications.

Key Features:

  • Powerful Brushless Motor – Ensures long runtime, high efficiency, and consistent performance.
