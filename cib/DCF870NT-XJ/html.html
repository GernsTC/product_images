The Impact Driver Designed for Quiet, Precision Work

Hydraulic impact drivers work differently from traditional models, they use a hydraulic pulse to deliver a gentler, quieter impact instead of the sharp, hammer-like strikes you’re used to. The DEWALT DCF870NT takes full advantage of this design, offering a significant reduction in noise without compromising the power needed for most fastening jobs.

While it may not match the raw torque of some standard impact drivers, the DCF870 delivers 56Nm of torque in a compact 100mm body, making it ideal for pros working in noise-sensitive or confined environments. Its hydraulic, oil-filled mechanism reduces vibration and noise without sacrificing control or durability.

Equipped with a brushless motor for longer tool life, a 3-mode control switch for precise fastening, and a super-bright 9-LED ring with a 20-minute flashlight mode, the DCF870 combines thoughtful features with excellent performance. If noise reduction and compact size matter in your work, the DEWALT DCF870 is a smart, quieter alternative worth serious consideration.

Key features:

  • Quiet hydraulic mechanism: Reduced impact noise and smoother operation
  • Low vibration: Enhanced comfort and control, even over long sessions
  • Compact 100mm length: Access tight spaces with ease
  • Brushless motor: Delivers power, efficiency, and extended tool life
  • 3-mode switch: Precise control for various applications
  • 9-LED ring with flashlight mode: 82 lumens of bright, even light; 20-minute hands-free illumination
  • Drop-and-load bit holder: Fast, one-handed bit changes
  • Part of the DEWALT 18V XR system: Fully compatible with over 250 cordless tools and batteries

Whether you're on the jobsite or in the workshop, the DEWALT DCF870NT delivers pro-level performance with less noise, less vibration, and more control.
